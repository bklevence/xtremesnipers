Games List

Current Rotation

  • OpenTTD - IYKYK - Because sometimes you need a break from shooting people to build virtual train empires. FOSS and it runs on a potato.
  • Supermarket Together - We like Trader Joe's SIM.
  • Battlefield 2 - The last REAL Battlefield game before EA ruined everything. 64-player battles, commander mode that actually mattered, and maps bigger than some countries. This is what peak performance looks like, kids. Everything after this was just corporate committee design.
  • Battlefield 6 Open Beta - EA's latest attempt to recapture the magic they accidentally deleted. We're testing it so you don't have to waste your bandwidth. Spoiler alert: it's still not as good as BF1942, but hey, at least the graphics are pretty while the servers crash.

Games That Have Left Us (RIP)

  • Battlefield 1942 - The OG! Still the best WW2 shooter ever made. None of this modern warfare nonsense.
  • America's Army - Free-to-play before it was cool. The government's recruitment tool that actually slapped!
  • Rise of Nations - For when we need a break from shooting and want to conquer civilizations instead.
  • Battlefield 2142 - Servers shut down. The future warfare we never got to finish.
  • Desert Combat Mod (BF1942) - The mod that was better than most full games. Ahead of its time.
  • Quake III Arena - Servers mostly dead. The arena shooter that demanded actual skill.
  • Unreal Tournament 2k4 - M-M-M-MONSTER KILL! The announcer will forever echo in our hearts.

Software, Abandoned (RIP)

  • GameSpy Arcade - The matchmaking service that connected us all. Gone but never forgotten. F in chat.
  • MSN Gaming Zone - Where legends were born and friendships were forged. Microsoft killed our childhood.
  • X-Fire - Medal.TV and Discord, well before Medal.TV and Discord...
